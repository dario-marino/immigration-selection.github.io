Select a mode above, set a threshold, then click any country on the map to see exactly which sex × education × age-at-arrival groups from that country clear the bar.
Sex × education × age-at-arrival groups computed from IPUMS USA / American Community Survey microdata (person-weighted). Income is mean total personal income (IPUMS INCTOT). Incarceration is the share of each group's ACS group-quarters population recorded in a correctional facility, per 100,000 — a standard proxy used in the immigration-crime literature since no U.S. dataset records crime by nativity directly. Groups built from fewer than 30 sampled records are marked unreliable and never count toward a country's qualifying total. GDP per capita is PPP, most recent year available; countries below a set threshold are shown in the excluded color regardless of group counts.
